About me

I’m an evolutionary biologist with broad interests in processes that shape biodiversity. During my PhD, I worked on larval morphology and behaviours, under the supervision of Benny Chan and Karen Chan, at Biodiversity Research Center, Academia Sinica, Taiwan. Currently I work on genomics in HK Tsai’s lab at Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, switching my research focus from phenotypic to sequence level.
