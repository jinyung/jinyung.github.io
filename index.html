About me

I’m an evolutionary biologist with broad interest in phenotypic evolution, particularly on larval morphology and behaviours. I recently finished my PhD under the supervision of Benny Chan and Karen Chan, at Biodiversity Research Center, Academia Sinica, Taiwan.

Interests

  • Functional morphology
  • Hydrodynamics of larval swimming
  • Origin and maintenance of biodiversity
