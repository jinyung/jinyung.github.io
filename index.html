About me

I’m an assistant professor at National Sun Yat-sen University, under the International Doctoral Program of Marine Science and Technology and jointly appointed at Department of Oceanography.

I’m an evolutionary biologist with broad interests in processes that shape biodiversity. During my PhD, I worked on larval morphology and behaviours, under the supervision of Benny Chan and Karen Chan, at Biodiversity Research Center, Academia Sinica.

I am also trained in bioinformatics and interested in studying biology with computational methods. During my postdoc, I work on genomics problem in HK Tsai’s lab using deep learning approaches at Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica.
